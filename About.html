About Us

Magabangejubane Holdings (MGH) is a proudly 100% black-owned and youth-led company, recognized as a B-BBEE Level 1 contributor. Founded in Richards Bay, MGH was established with a vision to provide reliable, innovative, and sustainable solutions across multiple sectors that drive South Africa’s growth. We specialize in logistics, civil engineering (CE), general building (GB), commodity handling, and agriculture, positioning ourselves as a versatile partner for industries such as mining, ports, construction, and farming. From side-tipper trucking and bulk material transport to infrastructure development and sustainable agribusiness, MGH delivers value with efficiency, integrity, and professionalism. At our core, we are driven by the commitment to empower communities and create opportunities. Through job creation, supplier development, and strategic partnerships, MGH continues to bridge the gap between industrial growth and social impact. With strong roots in Richards Bay and ambitions to compete nationally, our mission is clear: to connect industries, build sustainable futures, and set a new standard for excellence in South Africa’s logistics, construction, and agricultural sectors.

Our vision is to grow into one of the country’s leading logistics and commodity-handling enterprises, with strong roots in Richards Bay but the capacity to compete nationally and globally. Through strategic partnerships, supplier development initiatives, and community-focused projects, MGH is committed to creating jobs, empowering local businesses, and building sustainable futures.

Vision statement

To become a leading force in logistics and agribusiness across South Africa, driving growth through innovation, reliability, and empowerment — while building sustainable opportunities that uplift communities and connect industries nationally and globally.

Mission Statement

At Magabangejubane Holdings, our mission is to deliver reliable logistics, commodity handling, and agricultural solutions that create lasting value for our clients and communities. We are committed to operational excellence, innovation, and empowerment—driving growth while fostering sustainable development and inclusive opportunities.

Company Values

  1. Precision: We uphold the highest standards of accuracy and detail in all our projects, ensuring superior quality and performance.
  2. Safety: Safety is at the forefront of our operations. We are committed to maintaining a safe working environment for our employees, clients, and the communities we serve.
  3. Efficiency: We aim to deliver our services in a timely and cost-effective manner, optimizing resources and minimizing waste to provide the best value for our clients.
  4. Reliability: Our clients can count on us to be dependable and consistent in our service delivery, fostering long-term partnerships based on trust and mutual respect.
