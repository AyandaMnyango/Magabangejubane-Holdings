About Us

Magabangejubane Holdings (MGH) is a dynamic, 100% black-owned logistics and agribusiness company based in Richards Bay. Founded and led by a young entrepreneur, the company is proudly a B-BBEE Level 1 contributor, positioning itself as a trusted partner in South Africa’s industrial. Since inception, MGH has built its reputation on reliability, efficiency, and integrity, serving clients across mining, ports, agriculture, and construction. We specialize in side-tipper trucking, bulk commodity handling, heavy equipment services, and delivering value through operational excellence and innovation.

Our vision is to grow into one of the country’s leading logistics and commodity-handling enterprises, with strong roots in Richards Bay but the capacity to compete nationally and globally. Through strategic partnerships, supplier development initiatives, and community-focused projects, MGH is committed to creating jobs, empowering local businesses, and building sustainable futures.

Our Services

At Magabangejubane Holdings, we specialize in logistics and commodity handling, offering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective transport solutions tailored to meet client needs across Richards Bay and beyond.

  1. Roadworks and Maintenance
    • Re-graveling of Roads – restoring gravel surfaces for rural, municipal, and mining access roads.
    • Pothole Patching & Repairs – cost-effective and fast turnaround on damaged sections.
    • Shoulder Maintenance – reshaping and compacting road shoulders to improve safety.
    • Road Cleaning & Debris Removal – ensuring safer access routes.
  2. Small to Medium Earthworks
    • Site Clearing & Leveling – preparation for building or infrastructure projects.
    • Cut & Fill Operations – balancing earth movement for stable platforms.
    • Trenching for Services – small trenches for pipelines, drainage, or cabling.
